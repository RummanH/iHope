Privacy and Policy

Privacy policy 1: Data Collection and Usage

At iHope, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you use our medical app.

  • Information Collection: when you create an IHope account. We may collect personal information such as your name email address and contact details. Additionally, we may collect health related information that you voluntarily provide to us, including medical history and symptoms.
  • Usage of Information: We use the collected information to provide you with personalized medical services, including appointment scheduling, health monitoring, and symptom analysis. Your data may be used anonymously for statistical purposes to improve our apps performance and features.
  • Data Sharing: We understand the sensitivity of medical information. We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent unless required by law or for purposes related to your medical treatment. However, de-identified and aggregated data may be shared for medical research or analytics purposes.
  • Data Security: iHope takes appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We utilize industry standard security protocols and encryption techniques to ensure the confidentiality of our personal information.
  • User control: You Have the right to access, update or delete your personal information stored in our app. You can manage your data preferences and privacy settings within the iHope app or by contacting our support team

Privacy policy 2: Communication and Marketing

iHope is committed to respecting your privacy preferences and providing a secure communication environment. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information related to communication and marketing activities.

  • Communication challenge: iHope may contact you via email, SMS, or push notifications to provide important app updates, appointment reminders and relevant health-related information. You have the option to opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Marketing communication: we may send you promotional materials or offers related to our services or partners’ products if you have given your consent. You can unsubscribe from such marketing communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the communication or by contacting us.
  • Third-party Services: iHope may integrate with third-party services to enhance our app’s functionality. These services may have their own privacy policies and terms of use. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your personal information.
  • Minors: iHope is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 15. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor without parental consent, we will take appropriate steps to delete that information.
  • UPolicy updates: iHope may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our data handling practices. We encourage you to review the policy regularly. By continuing to use our app, you acknowledge and agree to the updated privacy policy.
